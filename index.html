SNAKE GAME

A simple idea with endless challenge. You control the snake, and every bite makes it grow.

Use the LEFT and RIGHT buttons to steer your snake as it moves forward on its own. Collect the food that appears, grow longer, and increase your score with every piece you eat.

Plan your turns carefully and avoid boxing yourself in. Keep moving, stay sharp, and see how long you can survive on your way to a new high score.
